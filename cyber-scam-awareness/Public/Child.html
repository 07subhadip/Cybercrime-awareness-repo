Child Rights

Children's rights are fundamental human rights that include the right to:

  • Protection: From violence, abuse, neglect, and exploitation
  • Education: To reach their full potential
  • Health: To access healthcare
  • Participation: To express opinions and be listened to
  • Development: To grow up in a safe, loving environment
  • Equality: To be free from discrimination

    • The offenses committed against a person below 18 years of age are considered crimes against children in India. Crimes against children can be physical, sexual, or emotional and include murder, kidnapping, sexual exploitation, abandonment, trafficking, etc.
